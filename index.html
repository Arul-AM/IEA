Who We Are

Established in 1978 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, Anna University is a globally renowned technical institution at the forefront of engineering, technology, and allied sciences. Known for its academic rigour, world-class research facilities, and strong industry-academia collaborations, the university serves as a hub for innovation and excellence in higher education.

The Industrial Engineering program at Anna University has a legacy of excellence dating back to 1973. Formally established as the Department of Industrial Engineering (DoIE) in 2002, we offer premier undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs, including specialised research in Quality Engineering and Management. Our mission is to integrate engineering principles, management strategies, and advanced data analytics.

The Society of Industrial Engineers is the vibrant, student-led heart of the department. We bridge the gap between classroom theory and industrial reality by hosting initiatives like the Industry Excellence Award, fostering a community where future IE leaders engage directly with professionals shaping today’s industrial landscape.

About IE Awards

The Department of Industrial Engineering and the Society of Industrial Engineers proudly present the Industry Excellence Award 2026.

The IE Awards serve as a distinguished platform to celebrate talent, innovation, and excellence within the Industrial Engineering community.

This premier initiative invites companies to participate and showcase their cutting-edge projects at no cost.

Theme for 2026

This year's theme, "ESG and Continuous Improvement – Shaping a Sustainable Future," underscores the increasing importance of responsible development and continuous refinement in today's industrial landscape.

In the modern era, true progress is defined not only by operational performance but also by an organisation's commitment to sustainability, ethical governance, and long-term societal impact. The theme encourages participants to move beyond basic compliance by strategically integrating high-level ESG objectives with disciplined Continuous Improvement (CI) methodologies.

Submissions are expected to demonstrate practical, data-driven applications of CI principles that deliver measurable and scalable impact across environmental, social, and governance dimensions. Through this focus, the initiative aims to highlight organisational leadership in sustainable operational excellence and contribute to shaping the future of contemporary industry.
